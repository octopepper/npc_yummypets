NFC Collar
Your pet's ID

Your pet’s ID with him everywhere:

The NFC collar by Yummypets is designed for pets. It is coupled with a smartphone application that allows you to embed the information on the pet and its owner.

Increase the chances of finding a lost pet:

It allows the owner to facilitate the process of finding the animal: you can report his disappearance to Yummypets community and geo-locate him when someone tags his collar.
