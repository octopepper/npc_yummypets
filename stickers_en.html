NFC Stickers
For the places labeled "Pet Friendly"

The first label referencing places accepting pets:

Fidolia is the Pet Friendly label that guarantees the acceptance of your pet in public places, hotels, restaurants, campgrounds, bars...

Crowdsourcing to reference locations:

Users can add locations from the platform or invite a manager to affix the label Pets Friendly in his establishment.

A platform dedicated to the use of NFC stickers:

With the Pets Friendly service, members have access to all relevant information to travel with their pet.
They can:

- Discover all the places that accept their animal

- Book a night in one of the hotels referenced “Label Fidolia”

- Discover new places to walk their companion

A chip that benefits to pet owners and professionals:

- Allow the community to express themselves: users can view and submit opinions on the site

- Offer deals to members when they tag a sticker

- Provide to professionals with access to relevant statistics
